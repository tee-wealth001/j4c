Who we are

Welcome to J4C

J4C Empowering the Next Generation is dedicated to identifying communities affected by youth violence and building effective networks that create meaningful change for young people. We have identified a strong link between school exclusions and gang involvement, and we aim to work proactively with both primary and secondary students to address this early on. Our approach includes training teachers to better manage challenging behaviour in the classroom and supporting parents, particularly those with children who have Special Educational Needs (SEN).

By positioning ourselves as key contributors in tackling local issues, we offer expert advice, deliver targeted training, and draw upon our existing knowledge and experience. This enables us to identify gaps in services and help organisations make more effective use of their resources. Our work contributes to reducing gang, gun, and knife-related fatalities and serious injuries, while also supporting individuals involved in such activities to transform their lives. Through direct engagement and referral to relevant partners, we provide pathways for those seeking change to access the help they need for a safer, more positive future

Our Services

CONSULTING

We provide personalised consulting services to support organisations in addressing gang violence through strategic interventions, community engagement, and evidence-based solutions. We work closely with stakeholders to develop sustainable programs that promote positive mental health, safety, resilience, and positive change.
Find Out More

EDUCATION AND TRAINING

We provide education and training programs to equip communities with the knowledge and skills needed to prevent gang involvement and support at-risk individuals. We also train teachers to deepen their understanding of challenging behaviour, helping them respond with confidence, compassion, and effective strategies in the classroom.
Find Out More

COUNSELLING AND MENTORING

We offer counselling and mentoring services to support individuals affected by gang violence, providing guidance, emotional support, and positive role models. Our programs help at-risk youth and community members build resilience, make choices, and develop pathways toward a safer and brighter future.
Find Out More

YOUTH PROGRAMS

Our youth programs provide safe spaces, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities to empower young people and steer them away from gang involvement. Through recreational activities, leadership training, and educational support, we help youth build confidence, develop positive relationships, and create a future full of possibilities.

About us

J4C we are committed to addressing and reducing the root causes of knife crime, gun crime, and gang-related violence in our communities. Our work is grounded in the belief that every young person, no matter their background or circumstances, deserves the opportunity to thrive, feel safe, and reach their full potential.

Our unique approach integrates lived experience, local knowledge, and strategic insight. We don’t just deliver services, we walk alongside young people and their families, offering real support, real change, and real hope.
