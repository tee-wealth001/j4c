Who we are

Welcome to J4C

J4C is a community interest company dedicated to supporting young people, families, and educators through mentorship, education, and advocacy. We strive to create a safe and empowering environment where young people can make positive changes in their lives and communities. By collaborating with schools, organizations, and community leaders, we develop impactful programs that address social challenges. Our commitment to inclusivity and resilience drives us to amplify the voices of young people and advocate for lasting solutions. Through direct support services, training, and policy advocacy, we work to build a brighter future for all.

Read more

What we do

Supporting Young People & Community

  1. Tackling youth violence and sexual exploitation through education and advocacy.
  2. Providing mentorship programs for young people.
  3. Creating platforms for youth expression and leadership.

Supporting Students & Education

  1. Development & social needs support.
  2. Assistance for parents with children with Special Educational Needs (SEN).
  3. Support for children with mental health issues.
  4. Vocational skills training and career guidance.

Community Safety & Development

  1. Preventing youth violence, gang violence, and knife crime.
  2. Reestablishing community support hubs, youth centers, and radio stations.
  3. Bringing back community wardens to enhance safety and support.

Our Services

CONSULTING

We provide personalized consulting services to support organizations in addressing gang violence through strategic interventions, community engagement, and evidence-based solutions. We work closely with stakeholders to develop sustainable programs that promote positive mental health, safety, resilience, and positive change.
Find Out More

EDUCATION AND TRAINING

We provide specialized education and training programs to equip communities with the knowledge and skills needed to prevent gang involvement and support at-risk individuals. Through workshops, mentorship, and capacity-building initiatives, we empower organizations and community leaders to create lasting positive change.
Find Out More

COUNSELLING AND MENTORING

We offer counselling and mentoring services to support individuals affected by gang violence, providing guidance, emotional support, and positive role models. Our programs help at-risk youth and community members build resilience, make choices, and develop pathways toward a safer and brighter future.
Find Out More

YOUTH PROGRAMS

Our youth programs provide safe spaces, mentorship, and skill-building opportunities to empower young people and steer them away from gang involvement. Through recreational activities, leadership training, and educational support, we help youth build confidence, develop positive relationships, and create a future full of possibilities.
